# FinGuide AI Pro

## Problem
Financial stress is one of the leading causes of academic dropouts among students. Most students lack awareness of their spending patterns, future financial risks, and how small changes today impact long-term stability.

## Solution
FinGuide AI Pro is an AI-powered financial health and decision intelligence platform for students. It analyzes income and expenses, calculates a financial health score, predicts future risk, and provides actionable AI insights to help students make better financial decisions.

- Savings goal awareness

## Tech Stack
- Frontend & UI: Streamlit (Python)
- Data Processing: Pandas, NumPy
- Machine Learning: Scikit-learn
- Visualization: Streamlit Charts

## How It Works
1. User enters monthly income, expenses, and savings goal
2. System calculates total expenses and savings
3. AI computes financial health score
4. Risk level and insights are generated
5. Expense trends are visualized and future prediction is shown

## AI for Good Impact
FinGuide AI Pro helps students identify financial risks early, reduce unnecessary spending, and plan savings responsibly. By preventing financial mismanagement, it supports student well-being and academic continuity.
